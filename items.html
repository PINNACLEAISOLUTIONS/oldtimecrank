The Crank Philosophy

At Old Time Crank, we believe that every scratch and patina tells a story. We specialize in restoring and selling authentic Edison phonographs, Victrolas, and rare wax cylinders.

Founded by enthusiasts for enthusiasts, we ensure every piece that leaves our shop is in working order, ready to fill your home with the warm, analog sounds of the past.
